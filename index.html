How to Play

Your job is to identify the exact note being played. You will hear a single target pitch. Use the virtual piano to locate it, click the key, and press Submit Answer.

Press R to replay the target pitch, and Space / Enter to submit your guess or advance to the next note. You can adjust the octave range anytime via Game Settings.

Game Settings ⚙️

Choose the octave range you wish to practice. Note, applying new settings will restart the game and reset the score!

Information

This game isolates your ability to recognize and replicate exact pitches. When a target pitch is played, try to internally sing or hold the note in your head before testing keys on the virtual piano.

Match the pitch perfectly to continue your streak! Use the keyboard shortcuts (R to replay, Space to submit/continue) for faster training.

You can also download a score card .png to track your progress over time!
